GunnHacks 2.0

Friday–Saturday, March 25–26, 2016, 7 pm–7 pm

Make, Build, Create & Learn. It’s GunnHacks 2.0, Gunn’s 24-hour high school hackathon.

Register

What is a hackathon?

Hackathons are where your crazy idea becomes reality. Students work in teams for 24 hours to create something amazing. Build anything—there are no restrictions on what you can create. Dive in and make the most incredible thing you can alongside fellow creators.

Who can attend?

All high school students from any high school are eligible. Unfortunately, we are unable to allow middle school students to attend this year.

What if I dont know how to code?

All students regardless of coding ability are welcome. Hackathons are a great place to learn, so come and build your first app or website! We'll also have workshops for you to learn something new.

What can I build?

Web, mobile, hardware, you name it. Any type of project is welcome. Projects will be judged based on creativity, technical difficulty, polish, and utility by a panel of industry judges.

How big should teams be? Can I hack alone?

We highly encourage you to build with a team. Teamwork is deeply rooted in the hacking ethos, and you'll have a better time building with your brightest peers. Teams should be at most five people. But don’t worry if you don’t have a team before the event—we will have a team-formation session Friday night.

How do I register?

Just click the button at the top! Registration closes on March 14.

How much does it cost?

GunnHacks will cost you zero, zil, nada. We will provide you with WiFi, meals, swag, and workspace for the entire event. You just need to get here.

What if I don't have a team or an idea?

No problem! Many hackers do not have a fully-formed team or hack concept before the event begins. We will be hosting team-forming activities after hacking begins to help you find the perfect match. If you're a first-time hacker, there will be many others looking for team members with whom you can work.

Where is GunnHacks?

GunnHacks 2.0 will be held in rooms N-115 & N-215 at Henry M. Gunn Senior High School (780 Arastradero Rd, Palo Alto, CA 94306). You can park in the main parking lot and look for signs to the event.

What is the schedule like?

Check back soon for a detailed schedule. The event will run from 7 pm on Friday to 7 pm on Saturday.

Do I have to work the whole time?

No, not at all! We will have dedicated sleeping areas and other fun activities for when you need to take a break.

When will I know if I my application was properly submitted?

You should have received an email after applying. Let us know at info@gunnhacks.com if this isn’t the case.

What should I bring?

All attendees are required to bring their valid student ID (or equivalent school ID) for admission. Bring laptops, phones, chargers, and any other tech you need for the weekend. Sleeping bags and toiletries are also highly recommended.

What if I have another question?

Please feel free to reach out to us at info@gunnhacks.com.
